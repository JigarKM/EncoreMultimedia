Make a true difference

Everyday stress too often takes a toll on our closest and most important relationships with spouses, families, and even with God. At Encore Academy, our new Master of Arts in Marriage & Family Therapy program can help you make a True Difference in the world by transforming lives, strengthening families and rebuilding relationships through counseling and faith.

A Christian foundation

What makes Encore Academy’s Master of Arts in Marriage & Family Therapy program distinct is our spiritual perspective and faith-based curriculum, integrating cutting-edge psychology with the timeless wisdom of God’s word.

classes in dallas and longview

Encore’s on-ground classes in Dallas and Longview and flexible online curriculum will prepare you for a rewarding career as a professional, faith-based counselor or marriage & family therapist.

A degree of difference

Marriage & Family Therapy is ranked as one of today’s top careers, and Encore’s MAMFT program provides the necessary academic requirements and clinical training you need to be LMFT or LPC license-eligible upon graduation. The profession of marriage and family therapy is projected to increase by 29% over the next decade (Bureau of Labor Statistics)..
